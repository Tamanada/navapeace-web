ABOUT THE MOVEMENT

Our Mission

NAVA PEACE is a global daily movement.
Every day, people from every country
make one simple commitment:

To choose peace.

Our goal is to demonstrate — in real time,
on a living world map — that humanity
chooses peace. Not as a dream.
As a daily act.

How It Works

1

Register

Join once. Share your country, age and gender. No account, no password, no email — your privacy is protected.

2

Choose Peace Every Day

Return each day and press the button. Your voice is counted on the global map. Every click is a real commitment.

3

Invite Friends

Receive a unique personal code. Share it via WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram or SMS. Every friend who joins expands the movement.

4

Earn Doves

Your peace impact grows daily. Earn a Dove for every day you act, and another for each day a friend you invited acts.

Our Principles

🔒

Anonymous

No name, no email. We only collect your country, age and gender to understand who chooses peace.

🌍

Universal

Open to every human being on Earth, from every country, culture and background.

📅

Daily

Peace is not a single act. Returning each day is what makes the movement real and powerful.

✨

Free

Always free. No subscriptions, no ads, no monetization. This is a movement, not a product.

📡

Transparent

All data is public and visible on the live world map. No hidden numbers, no manipulation.
