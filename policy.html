01

OUR MISSION

NAVA PEACE is a global movement built on a simple idea: if enough people consciously choose peace every single day, the world will change.

We believe in the collective power of daily intention. Every time you press the peace button, you add your voice to a global chorus that transcends borders, languages, religions, and politics.

This platform is free, anonymous, and open to every human being on Earth — regardless of nationality, age, gender, or belief. Peace is a universal value, and NAVA PEACE is its home.

02

COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

NAVA PEACE is a safe, inclusive, and peaceful space. By participating, you agree to uphold these values:

✅ What We Encourage

  • Sharing genuine, heartfelt peace messages with the global community
  • Inviting friends and family to join the movement
  • Daily engagement — one peace action per day, every day
  • Expressing solidarity with people of all backgrounds
  • Constructive, kind, and uplifting contributions

🚫 What Is Not Permitted

  • Hate speech — content targeting race, religion, gender, nationality, sexual orientation or disability
  • Violence or threats — any content promoting, glorifying or inciting violence
  • Political propaganda — partisan political messaging, election interference, or extremist ideology
  • Spam or manipulation — automated bots, fake accounts, or bulk fake referrals
  • Misinformation — deliberately false or misleading content
  • Commercial solicitation — unsolicited advertising or promotion of third-party products
  • Privacy violations — sharing personal data of others without consent
  • Content targeting minors — any inappropriate content involving people under 18

Violations of these guidelines may result in removal of your content and permanent exclusion from the platform.

PRIVACY & DATA

What We Collect

  • Anonymous device UID — a randomly generated string stored in your browser
  • Country, age group, gender — voluntarily provided during registration for statistical purposes
  • Daily peace action timestamps — to track your streak and global totals
  • Peace message — only if you choose to write and share one publicly
  • Referral code — if you invited someone or were invited by someone

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your IP address (no server-side logging)
  • Your location beyond the country you choose
  • Any financial or payment information
  • Any data from third parties or social networks

Data Storage

Data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL), a GDPR-compliant infrastructure provider hosted in the European Union. Your device UID data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage.

Your Rights

Since we collect no personal identifying information, individual data deletion requests are technically limited. However, you can clear your browser's localStorage at any time to remove all local data. To request global data removal, contact us.

Cookies

NAVA PEACE does not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We use only functional browser storage (localStorage) necessary for the App to work.

REFERRAL SYSTEM RULES

The NAVA PEACE referral system rewards users who spread the peace movement. Here are the rules:

Earning Doves

  • 1 Dove per day you choose peace (daily action)
  • 1 Dove for each new user who registers using your referral link
  • Referred users must complete registration to generate a dove reward

Prohibited Referral Behaviour

  • Creating fake accounts to self-refer
  • Using automated tools to generate referral clicks
  • Sharing referral links through spam, unsolicited messages, or deceptive means
  • Purchasing referral clicks or followers

Enforcement

NAVA PEACE monitors referral patterns for anomalies. Accounts detected abusing the referral system will have their dove score reset to zero and may be permanently excluded from the platform. We reserve the right to adjust dove scores at any time to correct manipulation.

No Monetary Value

Dove scores are symbolic indicators of your peace contribution. They currently have no monetary value and cannot be exchanged for money, goods, or services unless explicitly stated in a future programme.

CONTENT MODERATION

All peace messages shared publicly on NAVA PEACE are subject to moderation. Our approach:

How We Moderate

  • Community reporting — users can flag inappropriate content (coming soon)
  • Admin review — our team regularly reviews public messages
  • Automated detection — keyword filters for clearly harmful content

Our Standard

We aim to keep every message aligned with the spirit of peace: constructive, kind, inclusive, and hope-driven. Messages that express aggression, hatred, political extremism, or nihilism are removed — not because we fear debate, but because this platform is dedicated to a specific constructive intention.

Appeals

If your message was removed and you believe it was in error, you may contact us. We review all appeals within 7 business days.

Remember

Your peace message is your gift to the world. Make it something you would be proud to leave behind.
